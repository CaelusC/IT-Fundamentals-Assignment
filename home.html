My Hobbies

Im basically a full-time cat enthusiast, because lets face it, cats are the true rulers of the universe, and were just their humble servants. I spend my days researching every cat breed like Im writing a thesis on fluff, and my nights are filled with watching them knock stuff off counters with the precision of a seasoned ninja. Im convinced my cat is plotting world domination, but for now, Im just the one getting judged for not providing enough treats. I also have an unhealthy obsession with watching cat videos, which, let's be honest, is the true definition of self-care. If you need me, I’ll be the one asking my cat for life advice—because who wouldn’t trust the wisdom of a creature that naps 18 hours a day and still manages to look majestic?
